Important Fee Policies

  • Due Date: All monthly fees must be submitted by the 10th of every month.
  • Late Fee: A late fee fine of Rs. 50 per day will be charged after the due date.
  • Sibling Discount: A 15% discount on monthly tuition is available for the second child, and 20% for the third child enrolled from the same family.
  • Orphan Quota: Special fee concessions and free education quotas are available for deserving orphan students. Please contact the administration for details.
  • Refunds: The Admission Fee and Annual Charges are strictly non-refundable.
