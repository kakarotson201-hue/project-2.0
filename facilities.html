Our Facilities

Al-Madina is housed in a state-of-the-art, multi-storied building providing a safe, engaging, and resource-rich environment for all students.

Academic Excellence

  • Spacious, well-lighted & airy classrooms.
  • Computer lab with the latest PCs and multimedia projectors.
  • Well-stocked library featuring thousands of books, newspapers, and magazines.

Campus Life

  • Dining Hall with a capacity of 150 students at a time.
  • Boarding facility for over 100 students.
  • Sports store with a good stock of sports gear and equipment.
  • A grand, spacious mosque with a capacity of at least 500 at a time.

Health, Safety & Power

  • Casualty Centre for routine check-ups and immediate medical help.
  • Qualified doctor and medical assistant available round the clock.
  • Foolproof security system with private guards and Punjab Police.
  • Smoke detectors and firefighting equipment meeting international standards.
  • High-powered solar panels to handle the complex's entire electrical load.
