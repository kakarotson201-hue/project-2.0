Academic Curriculum

A comprehensive blend of modern contemporary education (Asria) and deeply rooted Islamic studies to ensure the holistic development of our students.

Hifz-ul-Quran (Dual Track)

  • Complete Memorization of the Holy Quran
  • Tajweed and Qira'at Rules
  • Integrated Middle Education: Students can pursue Hifz side-by-side with regular school subjects so they don't fall behind academically.
  • Customized daily timetable for balanced learning
  • Masnoon Duas and Islamic Etiquettes

Primary & Middle (Class 1-8)

  • English (Oxford/PTB Syllabus)
  • Urdu & Islamiat
  • Mathematics & General Science
  • Computer Science (Practical Lab Work)
  • Optional Hifz Track: Middle school students can opt to memorize the Quran alongside their standard daily classes.

Matriculation (9th & 10th)

  • Science Group: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Computer Science, Mathematics.
  • Arts Group: General Math, General Science, Islamic Studies, Education.
  • Compulsory: English, Urdu, Islamiat, Tarjuma-tul-Quran, Pak Studies.

Higher Secondary (11th & 12th)

  • F.Sc: Pre-Medical & Pre-Engineering
  • ICS: Computer Science, Math, Physics/Stats
  • I.Com: Accounting, Commerce, Economics
  • Shariah College: Specialized Islamic Jurisprudence & Arabic Literature

Board Affiliation & Examinations

Our Matriculation and Higher Secondary programs follow the official syllabus prescribed by the Punjab Textbook Board (PTB) and are fully affiliated with the regional Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BISE). Regular weekly and monthly assessments are conducted to ensure students are fully prepared for their final board examinations.
