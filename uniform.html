School Uniform

A neat and clean uniform reflects the discipline and standard of our institution. All students are required to strictly follow the uniform code.

Summer Uniform

  • White Shalwar Kameez (Standard Collar)
  • School ID Card with Blue Lanyard
  • Black formal shoes (without laces/with laces)
  • White socks
  • White Topi (Prayer Cap) for Hifz Students

Winter Uniform

  • White Shalwar Kameez
  • Navy Blue Sweater (V-Neck) or Blazer
  • School ID Card
  • Black formal shoes
  • White socks

Discipline & Grooming Rules

To maintain a focused and disciplined educational environment, please ensure the following:

  • Uniforms must be washed and properly ironed daily.
  • Hair must be kept short, neat, and properly trimmed. Fancy haircuts are strictly prohibited.
  • Nails must be trimmed short.
  • Wearing the official school ID card is mandatory at all times on campus.
  • Students arriving without the proper uniform may be subject to a fine or sent back home.
